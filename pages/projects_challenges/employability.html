Employability - Linked in

Linked in - what should I add to my profile?

Think about your technical skills — what could you add in?

- You can evidence these skills by completing skills assessments and by receiving skill endorsements

- If you're adding in soft skills, think about the industry and what skills are the most beneficial

- You can add up to 50 skills, but remember, sometimes less is more

- For some roles, you can also answer key interview questions to demonstrate your transferable skills

Linked in - what should I post on LinkedIn?

POST IDEAS

  • Ask questions
  • Offer some motivation
  • Shout out a successful project
  • Share insights from previous jobs/industries
  • Share a recent achievement
  • Start a content series
  • Create a top 10 list
  • Welcome new users
  • Share an insight from your favorite speaker or podcast
  • Share an experience of attending a job fair or event

Linked in - key things to remember

  1. See every diary entry as potential content
  2. Every post will have an audience - it's about how we find that
  3. Remember, if you are interested then someone else will be interested too
  4. Set yourself a challenge - posting more frequently
  5. Increase your reach through hashtags and tagging people that you know
